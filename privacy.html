Privacy Policy

At CrimeWatch, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure your data is protected.

Information We Collect

  • Anonymous Reports: We collect anonymous crime reports submitted by users. These reports contain no personally identifiable information unless voluntarily provided.
  • Device Information: We may collect technical information about the device used to access our platform (e.g., IP address, browser type) to improve our services.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to track website usage, improve user experience, and analyze trends. You can opt out by changing your browser settings.

How We Use the Information

  • Crime Reporting: Your reports are forwarded to law enforcement or relevant authorities anonymously to address community safety concerns.
  • Service Improvement: The information we collect helps us analyze usage patterns and improve the platform.
  • Communication: If you provide your contact information, we may use it to send updates or respond to your queries.

Data Security

We employ robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, firewalls, and secure access protocols.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except when required by law, or when it's essential for the service (e.g., forwarding crime reports to law enforcement). We do not sell your information to advertisers or marketers.

Your Rights

  • You can request to review, correct, or delete any personal information we may hold about you.
  • You have the right to opt-out of receiving communications from us.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When changes occur, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@crimewatch.com.
